Emergency & Critical Care

Our 24/7 Emergency Department is equipped to handle all urgent medical situations, staffed by experienced emergency physicians and nurses trained in critical care protocols.

  • Rapid response trauma care
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU)
  • Advanced life support and resuscitation

Surgical Services

We offer a wide range of surgical procedures utilizing minimally invasive techniques and the latest technology to ensure faster recovery and less discomfort.

  • General Surgery (appendectomy, hernia repair)
  • Orthopedic Surgery (joint replacement, arthroscopy)
  • Neurosurgery
  • Cardiothoracic Surgery

Maternity & Pediatrics

Dedicated care for mothers and children with a supportive environment and specialized pediatric services.

  • Prenatal and postnatal care
  • Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU)
  • Child wellness and immunizations

Cardiology

Comprehensive cardiac care including diagnostics, treatment, and rehabilitation to help patients maintain heart health.

  • Electrocardiograms (ECG/EKG)
  • Stress testing
  • Interventional cardiology (angioplasty, stents)
  • Cardiac rehabilitation programs

Diagnostics & Imaging

State-of-the-art diagnostic tools to assist in accurate and timely detection of medical conditions.

  • X-Ray, CT Scan, MRI
  • Ultrasound
  • Laboratory services including blood tests and pathology

Outpatient & Specialty Clinics

Convenient outpatient services across multiple specialties designed to meet ongoing health needs.

  • Dermatology
  • Endocrinology
  • Neurology
  • Physical therapy and rehabilitation
