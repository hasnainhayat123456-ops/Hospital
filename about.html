Our Mission

At Cambridge Hospital, our mission is to deliver compassionate, high-quality healthcare to every patient we serve. We strive to promote wellness, prevent illness, and restore health through innovative and evidence-based medical care.

Our Vision

To be a leading healthcare institution recognized nationally for excellence in patient care, medical education, and research. We envision a healthier community empowered by accessible and advanced healthcare services.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: Treating all patients with kindness and respect.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical standards in every action.
  • Excellence: Pursuing continuous improvement and innovation.
  • Collaboration: Working together with patients, families, and the community.
  • Safety: Ensuring the highest standards of patient safety and care quality.

Our History

Founded in 1980, Cambridge Hospital began as a small community clinic dedicated to providing basic health services. Over four decades, we have expanded into a full-service hospital featuring multiple specialized departments, cutting-edge medical technology, and a highly trained professional team.

Our growth is built on a foundation of trust, compassion, and a commitment to excellence in healthcare. Today, we serve thousands of patients annually and continue to pioneer advancements in medical treatments and patient care.

Community Engagement

Cambridge Hospital believes in giving back to the community through regular health fairs, vaccination drives, and educational workshops aimed at improving public health and awareness.

We partner with local organizations to address social determinants of health and strive to make healthcare accessible and equitable for all.
